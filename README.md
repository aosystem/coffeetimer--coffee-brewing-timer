# Coffee Brewing Timer (Hourglass Style) ☕⏳

A minimalist and atmospheric **Coffee Timer** built with **Flutter**. Rather than a precise laboratory instrument, this app is designed as a "digital hourglass" for coffee enthusiasts who appreciate the ritual and rhythm of brewing.

## 🌟 Concept & Features
- **Atmospheric Experience:** Features a visual representation of coffee dripping, serving as a companion to your brewing ritual.
- **Hourglass-style Measurement:** Measured in **1-minute intervals**, providing a relaxed pace for home baristas.
- **Minimalist Interface:** A distraction-free UI that lets you focus on the aroma and flow of your coffee.
- **Global Support:** Fully localized in **50+ languages**.

*Note: This app is intended for casual enjoyment and is not designed for tasks requiring scientific precision.*

## 💡 Best For...
- **Home Baristas:** Adding a visual touch to your pour-over or French press routine.
- **Coffee Lovers:** People who enjoy the slow-living aspect of brewing their own cup.
- **Daily Rituals:** Using it as a simple, visual guide for your morning coffee.

## 🛠 Technical Overview
- **Framework:** Flutter
- **Language:** Dart
- **Logic:** Simplified interval-based timer logic and smooth animation of brewing elements.

*Note: This repository contains the core logic and source code. Assets (images, sound effects, and localization files) are excluded to protect intellectual property.*
